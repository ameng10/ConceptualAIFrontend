Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

This policy explains how refunds and cancellations work for the ConceptualAI service at conceptual-ai.app, operated by ConceptualAI, Inc. Questions: admin@conceptual-ai.app.

1. During beta: the service is free

ConceptualAI is currently free during beta. No charges are made, so there is nothing to refund. The sections below describe how refunds and cancellations will work once usage-based credits — purchased outright or through monthly subscriptions — launch.

2. You see the price before anything runs

Every build is quoted in credits from your approved plan before it runs, and nothing is spent until you approve. You are never charged for work you did not approve at a price you did not see.

3. Failed builds cost nothing

A pipeline run that fails or is aborted consumes no credits. We do not bill for retries or for fixing errors the generator introduced.

4. Credit purchases

  • Unused credits: refundable on request within 14 days of purchase, to the original payment method.
  • Spent credits: non-refundable once consumed by a successful build, since the underlying compute and generation costs have been incurred — except as described in section 3 (failed builds are never charged) or where required by law.

5. Subscriptions

  • You can cancel a subscription at any time from your billing page or by emailing us.
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period; you keep access, and any credits the plan grants, until then.
  • We do not charge again after cancellation. Partial-period refunds are not provided unless required by law.

6. How to request a refund

Email admin@conceptual-ai.app from your account's email address with the purchase you're asking about. We respond within 5 business days, and approved refunds are returned to the original payment method.

7. Statutory rights

Nothing in this policy limits any refund or cancellation rights you have under the consumer-protection laws of your place of residence.
